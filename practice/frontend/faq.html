Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a service? +
You can book a service by selecting the required service, adding it to cart, and completing the booking form online.
Do you provide doorstep service? +
Yes, all our services are provided at your doorstep by trained technicians.
How can I track my service order? +
Login to your account and go to the “My Orders” section to track your service status.
What payment methods are available? +
Currently, payment can be made via UPI or cash after service completion.
What if I need to reschedule or cancel? +
You can contact our support team through the Contact page or WhatsApp for rescheduling or cancellation.
