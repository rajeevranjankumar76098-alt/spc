Refund & Cancellation Policy

Please read our refund and cancellation terms carefully

1. Service Cancellation

Customers may cancel a booked service before technician assignment without any cancellation charges.

2. Cancellation After Assignment

If cancellation is requested after a technician has been assigned, a nominal service charge may be applicable.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are applicable only if service could not be delivered due to unavailability of technician or other operational issues.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Inspection charges, completed services, and spare parts costs are non-refundable once the service has been delivered.

5. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days through the original payment method.

6. Payment Mode

Refunds will be credited back to the same UPI or payment method used during booking.

7. Contact for Refunds

For refund or cancellation requests, please contact our support team via phone or WhatsApp.
